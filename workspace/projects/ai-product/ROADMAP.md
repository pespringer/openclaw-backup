# AI Product Roadmap

## Objective
Ship one low-maintenance, sellable AI product aligned to Patrick's DevSecOps strengths.

## Phases

1. **Selection**
   - Choose idea using clear criteria.
2. **Validation**
   - Confirm buyer pain + willingness to pay.
3. **MVP Build**
   - Build smallest useful paid version.
4. **Pilot**
   - Get real users and collect feedback.
5. **Ship v1**
   - Public launch with clear onboarding/docs.

## Success Metrics (initial)

- First usable MVP shipped
- 3-5 real user trials
- At least 1 paid conversion (or strong pre-commit signal)
- <2 hrs/week maintenance during pilot period

## Constraints

- Prefer low operational overhead
- Avoid high-touch support models
- Favor straightforward deployment + backup
