## Decision
**Selected first product:** `AI-Powered Resume Optimizer`

## Why it won
- Highest weighted score in the decision matrix (**79**).
- Clearest buyer problem and value proposition among the current candidates.
- More straightforward path to a narrow MVP than the broader DevSecOps copilot idea.
- Better fit for the immediate goal of shipping something sellable without creating a high-maintenance support burden.

## Risks and Constraints
- The resume tool market is crowded, so positioning must be specific rather than generic.
- Product scope has to stay tight to avoid turning into a full career-platform build.
- Output quality has to feel materially better than a generic LLM prompt workflow.

## Rejected / Deferred Alternatives
- **DevSecOps Policy / Evidence Copilot** — strong domain fit, but still too broad and likely to require sharper niche definition before MVP selection.
- **Machine Learning Sports Analyzer** — interesting, but less aligned to Patrick's strongest professional edge and more likely to become a novelty product.

## Next Step
