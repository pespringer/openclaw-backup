# STORY-022 — Add research and validation desk to Mission Control

## Status
Backlog

## Story
As a user, I want a research and validation desk in Mission Control, so that product ideas, findings, objections, and evidence can be tracked alongside execution work.

## Why this matters
- Product success depends on validation, not just shipping tasks.
- Keeps research from disappearing into random notes.
- Connects customer learning to story prioritization.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Research items can be tracked separately from implementation stories.
- [ ] Findings, sources, and open questions are captured cleanly.
- [ ] Validation notes can be linked to projects/stories.
- [ ] Mission Control shows recent research/validation activity.

## Projected Work
- Estimate: L
- Approach:
  1. Define a research item model.
  2. Add UI for findings and sources.
  3. Link research items back to projects and stories.
  4. Keep execution board distinct from research desk.
- Dependencies: Documentation Hub / project portfolio direction
- Risks: mixing product discovery and execution too tightly.

## Deliverable
- Research and validation module for Mission Control.
